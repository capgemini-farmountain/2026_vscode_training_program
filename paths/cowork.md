# Path 3: Cowork & Automation

**For:** Business analysts, marketers, researchers, ops professionals
**Time:** ~24 hours across 12 sessions
**Modules:** M0 → M1 → C0 → C1 → C2 → C3 → C4

## What You'll Build
- Cowork concept map for your role
- Research assistant agent
- Office automation pipeline
- Content generation pipeline
- A complete cowork harness plugin your colleagues can install
